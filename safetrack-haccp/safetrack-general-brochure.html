Digital Temperature Monitoring

Five Stages. Every Check Logged.

SafeTrack covers the full journey — from the morning delivery to the last plate leaving the pass — every CCP captured, timestamped, and audit-ready.

Stage 01
Receiving
  • Delivery vehicle temp checked on arrival
  • Probe reading per incoming batch
  • Supplier name and reject/accept logged
Stage 02
Cold Storage
  • Cool room monitored continuously, 24/7
  • Alert the moment the reading starts climbing
  • Full overnight history, always on record
Sensor Monitored
Stage 03
Preparation
  • Batch-by-batch time tracking for cold prep
  • Alert when a batch has been out too long
  • High-risk items flagged: dairy, egg, mince
Stage 04
Cooking
  • Probe reading logged per dish or batch
  • High-risk items: pies, mince, pastry, sauces
  • Documented exceptions for medium-rare orders
Stage 05
Hot Holding & Service
  • Holding temp checked before service opens
  • Re-check log during extended service runs
  • Final probe before it leaves the pass
